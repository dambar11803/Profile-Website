INTRODUCTION
ATM MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is a Django based web application. The application is designed to manage the ATM CARDS for Banking System. It is especially used in Branch Offices for performing different actions regarding ATM CARDS.

OPERATIONS

  • Applying for New ATM CARD
  • Receiving & Storing New ATM CARD
  • Activating and Deactivating CARD
  • Renew, Re-Pin, Re-Issue of ATM CARD

ADVANTAGES

  • Easy to use
  • User-Friendly
  • Due to ORM, easy to query

APPLICATIONS

  • Banks & Finanacial Institutions
