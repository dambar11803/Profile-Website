INTRODUCTION
SUBSIDY INTEREST CALCULATION SYSTEM is a Django based web application. This web app is used to calculate the subsidy loan interest calculation. The Government of Nepal has provided various kinds of subsidy on certain types of Loan Module. For example 6% of total interest of Mahila Udhyamshil karja has been subsidized inorder to promote the local product. Similarly, Sainik Awash Karja is another kind of such facility where only 3.12% of total interest has been applied to the specilized customer.

OPERATIONS

  • Calculate the subsidized interest amount.
  • Calculate the remaining interest amount to be paid by the customer.

ADVANTAGES

  • Easy to use.
  • User-Friendly.
  • Saves a lot of time.

APPLICATIONS

  • Banks & Finanacial Institutions
